Title: Customer Feedback Dashboard.

	The Customer Feedback Dashboard is a comprehensive data visualization project developed using Power BI, a powerful business intelligence tool. This project aims to provide professionals and decision-makers with an interactive and informative dashboard that presents key Feedback metrics and insights for effective workforce management and decision-making for a specific Hotel.

The main objectives of the Customer Feedback Dashboard project include:

Data Integration: The project involves integrating data from various systems and sources.

Data Modeling and Transformation: Raw data is transformed and modeled to create a structured and organized data model suitable for analysis. This may involve data cleansing, data merging, and creating relationships between different feedback data sources.

Visualizations and Interactive Elements: The dashboard utilizes a variety of visualizations such as charts, graphs, heat maps, and tables to present analytics in a visually appealing and easy-to-understand manner. Interactive elements such as slicers, filters, and drill-through functionalities allow users to explore the data and gain deeper insights.

Workforce Demographics: This section provides visual representations.

The Customer Feedback Dashboard provides professionals and decision-makers with a centralized platform to analyze and understand critical feedback metrics, uncover insights, and make informed decisions to enhance workforce management, engagement, and organizational success for any given hotel.
